About

This website has a collection of clever hand drawn comic strips. If you are having a bad day and want to have a laugh you are in the right place and we hope it makes your day better! The comics spread over various genres, vampires, history, dogs driving cars, Godzilla playing with a cat, aliens, everyday humans and pizzas with feelings.

You can also create your own account which allows you to save your favourite cartoons and gain access to artist's email address to send them ideas and compliments.
